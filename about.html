About Us

Home / About us

George's Garden is a premium florist in offering flowers in store and flower delivery throughout Perth. The store has been around since the late 80’s, first in Leederville, then in Mt Hawthorn and now in Northbridge.

Over the years, we have developed a well-deserved reputation for stunning flowers, excellent service and reasonable prices. We also have a range of lovely indoor plants and gifts to go with your flowers.

We offer flower delivery Monday to Saturday across the Perth metropolitan area. Our friendly and competent couriers take very good care of their precious cargo and therefore make every effort to ensure that your flower or indoor plant delivery arrives at the right place and on time. Please see our Flower Delivery page for more information.

Our florists are all fully trained and highly skilled. We love doing what we do and, most importantly, we enjoy helping you to find the wonder and joy that flowers and plants can bring to your world and to special people in your life.

Our website is a guide to what we can do and an easy way for you to choose what you like. If you’d like to see more of what we do, have a look at our instagram page!

If you can’t find what you’re looking for on our website, we will very happy to help you find it. Please call us on (08) 9000 0000, contact us through the contact us or visit us in Northbridge.
